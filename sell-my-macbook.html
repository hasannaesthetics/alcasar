Specs Guide

How to find your MacBook specs

1

Check Model & RAM

Click the Apple menu () in the top-left corner of your screen and select About This Mac. Send us a screenshot of this popup.

2

Check Battery Health

Go to System Settings > Battery > Battery Health. Note down the Maximum Capacity % and the Condition (e.g. Normal, Service).

3

Sign out of iCloud

Before the meetup, remember to sign out of iCloud (Apple ID) and turn off Find My Mac to prepare the laptop for transfer.
